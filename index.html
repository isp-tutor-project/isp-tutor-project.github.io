Students: are you ready to begin your science fair poster journey? You will be taken through the very important first steps of doing a science fair project: Picking a good research question, doing research on your research question, and then creating a hypothesis for your research question. You will be focusing on an experiment about Crystal Growth in this lesson.

To do this, complete the following steps:

  1. Download or Print the Science Inquiry Worksheet before you start and fill it out during or after you complete each step.

  2. Complete the Good Research Question Lesson!
  3. Select a Research Question!
  4. Do background research about your research question.
  5. Create a hypothesis for your research question.
  6. Make sure your worksheet is complete and share it with your teacher!

Great work!
