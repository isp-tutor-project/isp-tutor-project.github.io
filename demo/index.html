Students: are you ready to begin your science fair poster journey?

You will be taken through the very important first steps of doing a science fair project: Picking a good research question, doing research on your research question, and then creating a hypothesis for your research question. You will be focusing on an experiment about Crystal Growth in this lesson.

To do this, you will need to follow each step below in order. When you're finished with a step, come back to this page and complete the next step.

First, download or print the Science Inquiry Worksheet before you start. For each step below, make sure to complete the worksheet questions for that step!

STEP 1: Complete the Good Research Question Lesson!

STEP 2: Select a Research Question!

STEP 3: Do background research about your research question.

STEP 4: Create a hypothesis for your research question.

STEP 5: Make sure your worksheet is complete and your changes are saved.
Then, share it with your teacher or parent!
