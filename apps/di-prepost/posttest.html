Scenario #1: Racing Toy Cars

Ethan wanted to see whether toy cars can go faster when they have which wheels or thin wheels.
He built two toy cars: one with thick wheels and one with thin wheels.
But everything else about the cars was the same.

Ethan made a ramp. He started with the toy car with the thick wheels from the rest at the top of the ramp and recorded how fast it was moving at the bottom of the ramp. He did this five times.

Then he did the same thing with the toy car with thin wheels.
He recorded the speed of this toy car at the bottom of the ramp five times.

Student Learning Experiment
